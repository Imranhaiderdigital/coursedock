# 1 : What is Amazon?

In this first lesson, we will understand what the Amazon platform is, how it operates as a global marketplace, and why it is the best platform to start an e-commerce business.

## Key Topics Covered:
1. **Amazon Marketplace:** How third-party sellers (like you) make up more than 60% of all physical sales on Amazon.
2. **Business Models:** Retail Arbitrage, Wholesale, and Private Label.
3. **Logistics:** Why Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) handles storage, shipping, and support, leaving you to focus on sourcing products.
