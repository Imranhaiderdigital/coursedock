# 2 : Amazon Analytics & Global Statistics

In this lesson, we will look at general statistics and analytics of the **Amazon Marketplace**. Understanding the sheer size of the platform, the number of buyers, and the share of third-party sellers helps us see the massive potential of selling on Amazon.

## Amazon Global Platform Statistics:
1. **Total Active Buyers:** Over **310 Million** active customer accounts worldwide.
2. **Prime Members:** Over **200 Million** paid Prime subscribers who enjoy fast, free delivery.
3. **Active Third-Party Sellers:** Approximately **2 Million** active sellers worldwide.
4. **Third-Party Sales Share:** Third-party sellers (independent businesses like you) account for **60%** of total physical units sold on Amazon!

---

## Amazon Marketplace Demographics & Sales Metrics

| Metric | Value | Significance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Active Customers** | 310,000,000+ | Huge ready-to-buy audience |
| **Prime Members** | 200,000,000+ | Customers who buy frequently |
| **Third-Party Seller Share** | 60% of total sales | Shows that small brands dominate |
| **Global Net Sales** | $570+ Billion / year | Massive capital exchange |

> [!NOTE]
> Even though there are millions of sellers, the 310 million active buyers mean there is constantly room for new private label products. Your goal is to capture a tiny fraction (e.g., 0.001%) of this market to run a highly successful business.
