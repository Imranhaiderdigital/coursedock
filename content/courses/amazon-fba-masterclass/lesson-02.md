# Advanced Product Research Strategies

Finding the right product is 80% of the success criteria for Amazon FBA. In this lesson, we will cover the metrics that define a winning product and look at research criteria.

## Product Selection Criteria

When researching products, aim for items that meet the following strict standards:

- **Sales Price:** Between $15 and $50 (sweet spot for impulsive buying).
- **Weight:** Under 2 lbs (reduces shipping costs significantly).
- **Size:** Fits inside a standard shoebox.
- **Reviews:** Top 5 sellers have fewer than 500 reviews.
- **Seasonality:** Steady sales year-round (avoid holiday-specific items).

> [!WARNING]
> Avoid highly complex mechanical or electronic items as they have a high rate of customer returns and defect claims!

### Product Sourcing Criteria Table
| Criteria | Target Metric | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sales Velocity** | 300+ units/month | Ensures healthy demand |
| **Profit Margin** | 30% or higher | Leaves room for PPC costs |
| **Sourcing Cost** | < 25% of retail | Maximizes ROI |
| **Fragility** | Non-breakable | Minimizes shipping damage |

## Tool-Assisted Product Research

Most professional Amazon sellers use software tools like **Jungle Scout** or **Helium 10** to spy on sales volume and search volumes.

### Helium 10 Chrome Extension Use Case
1. Go to Amazon.com and search for a keyword (e.g., "bamboo cutting board").
2. Run the extension on the results page.
3. Export the list to CSV, sorting by monthly revenue.
4. Filter out any sponsored listings (Ads).
5. Identify average price point and identify if the market is dominated by a single dominant brand.
