# Supplier Sourcing and Negotiation

Now that we have selected our product, we must find a manufacturer to produce it. In this lesson, we will look at using Alibaba to source suppliers safely and negotiate favorable terms.

## Sourcing on Alibaba

Alibaba is the largest marketplace for global trade. To avoid scams and find reliable suppliers, search for companies that have the following verification badges:

1. **Trade Assurance:** Protection service that covers your orders from payment to delivery.
2. **Verified Supplier:** A supplier whose company profile, capabilities, and facilities have been inspected by third-party verification companies.
3. **Gold Supplier:** Paid membership level showing supplier commitment.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Always pay through Alibaba's Trade Assurance platform. Never wire funds directly to a supplier's private bank account or use unverified payment methods outside of Alibaba.

### Sourcing Checklist
- [ ] Contact at least 5 different suppliers with a detailed RFP.
- [ ] Order samples from your top 3 choices.
- [ ] Hire an independent inspection agency (e.g. QIMA) to inspect production runs before final payment.
